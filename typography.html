CUSTOM TYPEFACE

Cyberpunk Edgerunners' logo uses a handcrafted typeface characterizing the hi-tech, technologically advanced yet bleak future. It features a glitchy, neon, and distorted style and the edges of each letter is jagged and rugged, reflecting the cutthroat nature of the Cyberpunk universe.

FUTURISTIC HOLO-UI

Unlike the Cyberpunk Edgerunners logo, the hi-tech holographic interface that the characters interact it actually uses a standard font, it's called the "Rajdhani" typeface, designed by Indian Type Foundry. Fun Fact: THIS is Rajdhani that you are and have BEEN reading, and it's the typeface used in all the paragraphs of this webpage.

NEON PALETTE

The Edgerunners palette captures the soul of Night City - glowing neon nights against the dark urban shadows. Bright pinks, cyans, and yellows pop against black or muted greys, symbolising life and rebellion within the chaos and cruelty of Night City.
