TRANSHUMANISM

With Edgerunners being set in the Cyberpunk universe, it's easy to see Transhumanism's influence. Transhumanism advocates for the transformation of the human condition by using technology to enhance intellect and physiology, inspiring the Cyberpunk universe's part human and part machine visuals.

HI-TECH FASHION

Edgerunners' fashion blends streetwear with hi-tech gear - metaallic fabrics, neon accents, tactical belts, and glowing accessories. Outfits are a form of self-expression in a world where looks are everything. Much like Night City itself, keeping up appearances is how the strong survive.

IT'S GETTING GLITCHY!

Movement in Edgerunners reflects an unstable world - flickering lights, trailing frames, glitchy transitions, and heart-racing pulses. When combined together, it creates intensity and evokes emotion (mostly tension), mirroring the struggle of the main characters' fight for survival against Night City's cruelty.
